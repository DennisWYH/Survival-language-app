About the Site

This site provides real-life foreign language materials sourced from everyday contexts. Unlike traditional tools that mechanically prompt you to recite new vocabulary or sentences, this platform offers real-life puzzles for you to solve, enhancing comprehension and enjoyment simultaneously.

You might come across a job posting at a supermarket seeking more staff or a local municipality announcement informing you about new trash bins for kitchen waste disposal.

I hold the belief that language learning becomes more engaging and meaningful when connected to real-life applications.

Flash/Done/Pass/Repeat

These are concepts borrowed from climbing world.

Flash, means that you understand the card problem on the first attemp without any additional help.

Done, means that you understand the card problem with some help from dictionary look up.

Pass, means that the card is by any chance too difficulty for you and it's a hassle to look up all the words, so you don't want to work on it for the moment.

Repeat, means that you've done the problem before, and now you're repeating the exercise for fun.
